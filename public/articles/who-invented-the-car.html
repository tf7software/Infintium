Who invented the car?

It's tricky to pinpoint one inventor for the car as it was a product of many innovations and contributions over time. However, Karl Benz is widely credited as the inventor of the first gasoline-powered automobile in 1886.

He built the Benz Patent Motorwagen, which is considered the first true automobile. However, other inventors like Gottlieb Daimler were also working on similar vehicles at the same time.
